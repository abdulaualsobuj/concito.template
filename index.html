About Us

Concito is an integrated communications firm based out of Dhaka, Bangladesh. Concito was one of the first public relations agency in the country, being formed in 2009, and has, since then expanded services to include integrated communications solutions. Our services include public relations, government relations, internal communications, digital marketing, event management content production and much more. Over the years Concito has worked with some of the best brands and organizations from across a wide range of industries. Through a renewed integrated approach, Concito continues to remain one of the leading names in PR and communications in Bangladesh.
